# Antigravity Research Labs — Company Policy

## Remote Work Policy
Antigravity Research Labs allows all employees to work remotely from any location worldwide.
Employees may work from any altitude, including mountain tops, airplanes, and space stations.
The only requirement is a stable internet connection for team meetings.

## Vacation Policy
All employees receive 30 days of paid vacation per year.
Unused vacation days roll over to the next year, up to a maximum of 45 days.

## Technology Stack
The engineering team primarily uses Python, LangChain, and Google Gemini for AI projects.
All code must be reviewed by at least two team members before merging.
The company uses ChromaDB for internal vector search applications.

## Office Locations
- Headquarters: San Francisco, California
- European Office: Berlin, Germany
- Asia-Pacific Office: Tokyo, Japan
